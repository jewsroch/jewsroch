Leland has a small but varied selection of housing options. Below is information on house rentals, hotels and cheaper accomidation if you're on a budget. Because Leland is a vacation destination, we recommend reserving your housing as soon as possible. Thank you!

Rentals

  • Leland Rentals
    This website has a great list of different houses available for rent in Leland.
  • VRBO
  • Bed & Breakfast - Use this website to search for Bed & Breakfasts and Inns in the area.
  • 301 South Grand Avenue - The Korrochs and the Jewsburys stayed here last summer. Great house! Great Location!

Hotels

  • Leland Lodge - Great place to stay in Leland between Lake Leleanau and Lake Michigan. They rent by the room and they have cabins. Great location!
    Address: 565 Pearl Street, Leland, MI
  • The Homestead Resort - Fantastic resort on Lake Michigan about 20 minutes from Leland. They have a huge variety of rental options from condos to hotel rooms. Kate and her family have spent many summer there.
    Address: Wood Ridge Road, Glen Arbor, MI
  • Snow Bird Inn - A sweet little bed and breakfast about a 5 minute drive from downtown Leland.
    Address: Leland, MI
  • Whaleback Inn - This small resort is about a 5 minute drive from Leland and has both rooms and cottages available.
    Address: 1757 North Manitou Trail, M-22, Leland, MI

Budget

  • Super 8 Motel
    Address: 1870 US Highway 31, Traverse City, MI
  • Econo Lodge - Marty, Chad and Kate have all stayed here at various times when they were limited by student budgets! A great price! Not too far away.
    Address: 1065 M 37 South, Traverse City, MI
  • Baymont Inn & Suites
    Address: 2326 US 31 South, Traverse City, MI 49684 US
